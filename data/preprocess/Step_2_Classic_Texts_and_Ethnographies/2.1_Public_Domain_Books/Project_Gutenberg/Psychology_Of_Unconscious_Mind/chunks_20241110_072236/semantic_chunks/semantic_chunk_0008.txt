INTRODUCTION

The following text is a collection of thoughts and ideas on the symbolism of the mother, as seen in various myths and legends. The author explores the different aspects of the mother archetype, including her role as a symbol of rebirth, her connection to the unconscious mind, and her relationship to the hero's journey.

The text is divided into three sections: THE BATTLE FOR DELIVERANCE FROM THE MOTHER, THE DUAL MOTHER ROLE, and THE SACRIFICE. Each section delves into a different aspect of the mother archetype, using examples from mythology and psychology to illustrate the author's points.

Throughout the text, the author draws on a range of sources, including the works of Sigmund Freud and other psychoanalysts, as well as mythological and literary references. The author's goal is to provide a comprehensive understanding of the mother archetype and its significance in human psychology.
