INTRODUCTION

The following text is a collection of notes and ideas on the symbolism of the mother, as seen in various mythologies and cultures. The author, Guglielmo Ferrero, explores the concept of the mother as a symbol of rebirth, death, and transformation, and how it relates to the human psyche.

The text is divided into three parts: the first part, which includes the introduction, the second part, which explores the battle for deliverance from the mother, and the third part, which examines the sacrifice.
