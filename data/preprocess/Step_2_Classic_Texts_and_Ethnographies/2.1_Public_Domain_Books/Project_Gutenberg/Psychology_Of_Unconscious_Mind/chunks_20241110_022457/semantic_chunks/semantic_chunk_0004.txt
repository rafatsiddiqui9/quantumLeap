The Reveal of Oedipus' True Identity

Oedipus sent his brother-in-law, Creon, to find the murderer of Laius. Creon discovered Oedipus and Laius on the road to Corinth, where they were trying to kill each other. However, Oedipus intervened and saved Laius' life. This event led to the prophecy that Oedipus would kill his father and marry his mother.
